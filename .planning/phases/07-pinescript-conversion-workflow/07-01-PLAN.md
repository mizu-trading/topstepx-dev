<objective>
Create the adapt-pinescript workflow that converts TradingView PineScript strategies into live-tradeable TopStepX bots with mandatory repainting audit, multi-timeframe lookahead bias audit, and full safety guardrails.

Purpose: PineScript strategies appear profitable in TradingView backtests but can trade on signals that would NOT have existed in real-time (repainting). Without this workflow, PineScript-to-bot conversion is manual and dangerous -- repainting survives conversion, auto-reversal creates double-sized positions, and bar data infrastructure is missing. This workflow makes conversion systematic, safe, and auditable by enforcing SAF-04 before any code generation.

Output: New file `topstepx/workflows/adapt-pinescript.md` (~700-1000 lines) following the established TSX workflow XML tag structure with 11 numbered steps, inline PineScript mapping references, repainting and MTF audit gates, and mandatory SAF-01 through SAF-05 safety verification.
</objective>
